Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MultiversoDigital ("we", "us", or "the App") handles your information. The App is a private administrative tool designed for Windows to manage and post content directly to TikTok.

1. Data Collection

We only collect the minimum data required to perform the Direct Post functionality via the TikTok API:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access and Refresh tokens to authorize your posts.
  • Basic Profile Info: Your TikTok username and avatar to identify the active session.

2. Data Usage & Storage (Iron Law of Stability)

In accordance with our core development principles, data integrity is our priority:
  • Local Persistence: All authentication tokens are stored locally on your Windows machine using Hive.
  • Self-Healing: If a local storage box is corrupted or destroyed, the App is programmed to reconstruct it immediately to prevent crashes.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not host your video files. They are transferred directly from your local device to TikTok's servers.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties. It is only transmitted to TikTok's official endpoints to fulfill your request to publish content.

4. Security

The App is built to be immune to silent errors. All API interactions are strictly typed, and security tokens are handled as objects to prevent unauthorized access during the execution flow.
